Itinerary

Who knows the next time one may find themselves in Greece - so it's important to come prepared with an plan.

Here is an example schedule for one of my days in Athens.

Time Activity
8am-9am Hotel breakfast
9am-9:30am Walk to Acropolis
930am-12:30pm Sight see at the Acropolis

Tip! Select a hotel close to the Acropolis. Athens is a very walkable city. Most day excursions offer hotel pickup - but they tend to offer that for those in the inner-city.
